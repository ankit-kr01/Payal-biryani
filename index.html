About Payal Biryani

Serving authentic, flavorful biryani to the heart of Bhubaneswar

Traditional Recipes, Modern Excellence

Payal Biryani is a fast-growing local restaurant based in Bhubaneswar, India, known for serving authentic, flavorful, and affordable biryani. We specialize in freshly prepared chicken and veg biryani made with premium basmati rice, rich spices, and traditional cooking techniques that deliver unforgettable taste in every bite.

Our menu includes a variety of veg and non-veg dishes, starters, and customer-favorite items like Chicken Lollipop and Paneer specialties. At Payal Biryani, we focus on quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction.
